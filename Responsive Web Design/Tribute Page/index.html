Udyr

The Spirit Walker and almighty God of the Jungle.

Some of his great deeds:

  • Created Runeterra.
  • Granted the power of the spirits to the Ionians who would become the first Vastayashai'rei, so they could win the war against the Titans.
  • Gave birth to the wild Freljordan Demi-gods, who sowrn to protect him with their lives. They are his loyal guards until this day.
  • Ended the war of the Three Sisters and imprisioned the Watchers at the bottom of the Howling Abiss.
  • Maded the Sun Disc and instructed the Shurimans on how to use it to create the Ascended. This would lead to The Golden Age of the Shuriman Empire.
  • Sealed the Great Rift at Icathia, halting the spread of the Void.
  • Created the World Runes, but the humans were not prepared to recive shuch immense power, so he hided them in a safe place.
  • Stopped the Noxian invasion of Ionia all by himself, when he was traveling with his bald friend Lee Sin.
